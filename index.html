Privacy Policy for InvoiceIQ AI

Last Updated: June 2026

InvoiceIQ AI is developed by KNK LTD with the core purpose of helping users manage their invoices easily, safely, and privately. Our app helps users import PDF and image-type invoices, store them locally on their device, extract useful invoice information, and make invoice searching and management easier.

1. Core Purpose of the App

InvoiceIQ AI is designed to help users manage invoices in a simple and organized way. Users can import invoice PDFs or invoice images into the app. The app extracts important invoice details so users can easily search, find, categorize, and manage their invoices later.

2. Local Data Storage

Invoice files and extracted invoice data are stored locally on the user's device. We do not upload, sell, rent, or share your invoice documents with any third party.

3. Use of AI

InvoiceIQ AI uses AI only to help categorize invoice data and make invoice management easier. AI is used only for app functionality, such as identifying invoice categories and organizing extracted invoice information. We do not use your invoice data for advertising, selling, or sharing with third parties.

4. Information We May Collect

InvoiceIQ AI may collect basic account information such as email address and password for login and authentication purposes. Your email and password are not shared with any third party.

5. Email and Password Privacy

Your email and password are used only for secure login and account access. We do not sell, rent, or share your login details with any third party.

6. Invoice Data Privacy

Your invoice data belongs to you. InvoiceIQ AI does not share your invoice documents, invoice images, extracted invoice data, or category information with any third party. The purpose of invoice extraction is only to help you search and manage your invoices more easily.

7. Third-Party Services

8. Payments and Subscriptions

9. Advertisements
